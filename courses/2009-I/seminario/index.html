Course Description

Objective

  • To develop a critical, creative and committed attitude in the graduated student, that allows her/him to assume responsibly the research work.
  • To instruct students in the use of the fundamental research tools that allow her/him to write a research proposal, to develop a research work, to present research results in the form of research report (thesis), scientific paper and poster.
  • To provide opportunities for students to present their research results and to receive feedback from the other seminar's attendants.

Methodology

  • Students must present research reports according to their level:
    • MEng students:
      • Thesis project (Spanish)
      • Poster  and scientific paper with concrete results from the thesis' research
      • Thesis draft (Spanish)
    • PhD students:
      • State of the art paper (including annotated bibliography)
      • Poster and scientific paper with concrete results from the thesis' research
      • Thesis project draft
  • Scientific papers will be orally presented
