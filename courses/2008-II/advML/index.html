Course Description

Objective

The main goal of this independent-study course is to study some advanced topics in Machine Learning (ML) including: semi-supervised learning, learning to rank, kernels and text mining.

Methodology

  • The course is based on a reading material set that is mainly composed of research papers and book  chapters.
  • Each paper/chapter is assigned to a session. All the students are supposed to read it. The presenter is randomly chosen.
  • Each student must propose a project related to the course content. The student must present an intermediate report and a final report.
  • Additionally, some assignments may be given along the semester.

Contents

Session Activity
1. Introduction to semi-supervised learning  [Chapelle06] Chap 1 and 2
2. Low-density separation for semi-supervised learning  [Chapelle06] Chap 6 and 10
3. Graph based methods for semi-supervised learning  [Chapelle06] Chap 11 or 12
4. Change of representation for for semi-supervised learning  [Chapelle06] Chap 15 or 17
5. Learning to rank 1 [Herbrich00] and [Joachims02]
6. Learning to rank 2 [Burges05] and [Cao07]
7. Parallel machine learning[Song08] and [Chu07]
8. Kernel design[SC04] Chap 9
9. Kernels for documents and structured data[SC04] Chap 11
10. Image kernels
11. Machine learning for text

    • Final report 15%
  • Other 10%
